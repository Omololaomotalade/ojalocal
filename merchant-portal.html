🟢 FIFO — First In, First Out
Oldest stock sells first. Best for perishables (fruits, veg, fish) and livestock — live animals cost more to hold each day.
🟡 LIFO — Last In, First Out
Newest stock sells first. Best for shelf-stable goods (grains, spices) when prices are rising — reduces taxable profit.
